Privacy Policy

Kerr Sundries is committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the laws and business standards of New South Wales (NSW), Australia. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you interact with our online retail shop.

1. What Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information from you:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number

This information is collected when you join our email and phone list, make a purchase, or otherwise interact with our website.

2. Purpose of Collection

We collect your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To send you updates, promotions, and marketing communications by email or phone
  • To provide customer support and respond to your enquiries
  • To improve our products, services, and website

We will not use your information for any purpose other than those stated above without your consent.

3. How We Collect and Store Information

Your personal information is collected directly from you via our website forms or when you communicate with us. We store your information securely using industry-standard security measures to protect it from unauthorised access, modification, disclosure, or loss.

4. Disclosure of Personal Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. Your information may be disclosed to service providers who assist us in operating our website and conducting our business, but only where they comply with Australian privacy standards. We do not transfer your personal information overseas unless required and with your consent.

5. Access, Correction, and Unsubscribing

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request corrections to your information if it is inaccurate or out of date
  • Unsubscribe from our email and phone list at any time by following the instructions in our communications or contacting us directly

We will process unsubscribe and correction requests promptly.

6. Data Retention and Destruction

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described above or as required by law. When your information is no longer needed, we will securely destroy or de-identify it.

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from misuse, interference, loss, unauthorised access, modification, or disclosure. This includes using secure servers, encryption, and regular security reviews.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal obligations. The latest version will always be available on our website.

By using our website or providing your personal information, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

This policy is designed to comply with the Privacy and Personal Information Protection Act 1998 (NSW), the Australian Privacy Principles under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth), and best practice for NSW retail businesses.
